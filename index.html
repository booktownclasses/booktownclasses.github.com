Frequently Asked Questions

What is the batch size?
Maximum 16 students per batch to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. This small batch size allows our faculty to focus on each student's individual learning needs.
What are the fees structure?
Fee varies by grade and program. We offer competitive pricing with flexible payment options. Contact us for a personalized fee structure and scholarship information. Scholarships available for meritorious students.
Is there a trial class available?
Yes, we offer a free demo class for all new students. Fill out the contact form to schedule your trial session and experience our teaching methodology firsthand.
What teaching methodology do you follow?
We follow the BookTown Method™ — a unique blend of concept clarity, regular assessments, personalized feedback, and smart goal setting. Our approach ensures deep understanding rather than rote learning.
